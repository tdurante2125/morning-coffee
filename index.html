My Morning Coffe

Rise and Shine! Goodmorning, to get your day started off right lets go get ourselves a cup of joe.

Obviously the first step to having morning coffee is to get the coffee brewing. The steps are simple.

  1. Add water
  2. Add coffee grain (using a filter)
  3. Press the "Brew Now" button

Congratulations! You're coffee is brewing, now we just need to wait for it to be ready.
